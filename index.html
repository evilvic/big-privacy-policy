Welcome to Big ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience when using our mobile application ("Big," "the app," or "our services"). Big is designed with your privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or process any personal data on our servers. All information you input into the app, including decks, cards, and any customization options, is stored locally on your device.

2.1 Information You Provide

We do not automatically collect any information about you or your device.

3. How We Use Information

As we do not collect or store any of your information on our servers, we do not use your data for any purpose beyond the functionality provided within the app on your local device.

All data is stored locally on your device. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data against accidental or unlawful destruction, loss, alteration, unauthorized disclosure, or access. However, please note that no system is ever completely secure.

5. Your Rights and Choices

As all your data is stored locally on your device, you have full control over it. You can access, modify, or delete your data at any time within the app.

6. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will take steps to delete such information.
