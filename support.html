Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

  • How do I create a new deck? You can create a new deck by tapping on the "Create Deck" button in the app. Then, customize it with your preferred background and color scheme.
  • How do I delete a deck? To delete a deck, long-press on the deck in your list to enter edit mode, and then select the "Delete" option.
  • Can I change the text size? The text size is automatically adjusted to fit the card for optimal readability. Unfortunately, text size customization is not available at this time.
